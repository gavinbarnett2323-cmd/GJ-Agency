Our Story

Built by a Kid
Who Wanted to Earn It.

GJ Agency was started by Gavin, a Sacramento-area college student who decided that instead of waiting for opportunity, he'd build it himself. What started as a car detailing side hustle turned into a full-service home and auto company.

When you hire GJ Agency, you're not calling a faceless corporation. You're calling a driven young entrepreneur who personally shows up, personally does the work, and personally guarantees you'll love it.

We're building our reputation one job at a time — and that means we can't afford to cut corners. Your satisfaction isn't just a policy. It's how we survive and grow.
