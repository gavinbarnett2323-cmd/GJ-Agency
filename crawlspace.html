Why It Matters

PROTECT WHAT'S
UNDER YOUR HOME

MOISTURE IS THE
SILENT PROBLEM.

Most homeowners never look under their house. That's exactly why crawlspace moisture damage gets so bad before anyone notices — by the time it shows up as a musty smell, warped floors, or high energy bills, the damage is already done.

A properly installed vapor barrier keeps ground moisture from evaporating up into your home's structure. It's one of the most cost-effective things you can do for the long-term health of your house.

✦
Stops Mold Before It Starts Mold needs moisture to grow. Cut off the moisture source under your home and you stop the problem at the root.
✦
Protects Wood Framing & Joists Constant moisture causes wood rot in the structural members that hold your floors up. A barrier adds years to their life.
✦
Lowers Heating & Cooling Costs Moisture in the crawlspace makes your HVAC work harder. A dry crawlspace means a more energy-efficient home.
✦
Required for Most Home Sales Home inspectors flag missing or damaged vapor barriers. Getting it done now saves headaches when you sell.
